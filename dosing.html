What Is The Dosage
Of ZYRTEC?

Adults and adolescents above 12 years old

The usual dose of ZYRTEC is one 10 mg tablet, once a day.1

Children 6–12 years

The recommended dose is 5 mg twice daily (morning and evening).
The 10 mg tablet can be split into two equal doses.1

How To Administer ZYRTEC?

Swallow the whole tablet, with a glass of water.1

And you can divide the tablet into two equal doses.1

ZYRTEC With Food &/
Or Drinks

  • Take ZYRTEC with food and with glass of water.2
Doses are subject to the doctor's recommendation
Consult your doctor/pharmacist before using ZYRTEC
