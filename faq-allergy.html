• Managing Allergies

Frequently Asked Questions

Allergy occurs when your immune system overreacts to something that is harmless to most people but triggers a reaction in anyone sensitive to it.1

Allergic symptoms vary from person to person. Here are some symptoms: runny nose, stuffy nose, sneezing, watery eyes, rashes, & headache.2

There are many types but categorized as Seasonal & Perennial.3

Avoiding things that you're allergic to is your best option to manage allergies. Your healthcare provider might recommend that you take certain medications daily or get specific treatments regularly. Some medications as antihistamines.4

You can manage allergy through some simple actions such as staying away from known triggers, keeping a diary or note, &/or wearing a medical alert bracelet.2

Consult your doctor/pharmacist before using the product.
